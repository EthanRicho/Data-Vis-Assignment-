DATA VISUALISATION - ALCOHOL CONSUMPTION

Alcohol is a recreational drug that affects the way our body functions and can be addictive if not consumed in moderation.

This website presents visualized charts that will go into detail on how alcoholism has affected Australians

The primary audience for these interconnected sets of data is Australia’s Department of Health and Aged Care, alongside policy makers who will work together to enforce public policies, economic decisions, and social welfare programs to effectively reduce alcohol consumption. Based on the visualizations, the government can raise public awareness of the harmful effects of alcohol and how it ties to the country’s current rate of unemployment. In a similar fashion, healthcare professionals will also find value in this information as a secondary audience.
